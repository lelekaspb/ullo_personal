The green initiative

For us at Ullo, it is important to give back where we can. We value sustainable and innovative solutions, and therefore we have taken various initiatives that we believe are some important steps towards a better world and future. One of the measures is to compensate for CO2. In collaboration with Ecologi, Ullo has made it possible to plant a tree for each product sold. This means that for every product you buy, Ullo plants a tree! Of course it's Ullo who pays, so don't worry, it's not something that makes it more expensive for you.

Visit our forest

It is important that you as a customer know that there is action behind our words when we say we plant one tree per unit sold. Thanks to a collaboration with Ecologi, you can follow your trees and the rest of our forest.

Packaging

Our packages are made of FSC-certified materials. FSC is an international non-profit labeling scheme for wood and paper which ensures that more of nature will never be used than it can reproduce.
